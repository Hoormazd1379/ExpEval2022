The camelCase vs kebab-case Experiment

Hoormazd Pirayeshfar & Delara Lomen

Experimentation & Evaluation 2023

Project 2

Hello and thank you for participating in our experiment!


Goal of the Experiment:

The goal of this experiment is to understand how different identifier styles (camelCase and kebab-case) affect readability and recognition in code.


What You Are Supposed to Do:

You will be presented with short sentences and a set of identifiers written in either camelCase or kebab-case. Your task is to select the identifier that corresponds to the given sentence. We will track the time it takes for you to make each selection and whether it was correct or incorrect.

Please read the instructions carefully and follow the prompts as you proceed through the experiment tasks.


Thank you for your participation!


Note: The collected data will be used for research purposes only.
